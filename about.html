OUR STORY

2020

The Beginning

RoboPig started as a classroom project when our founder, a teacher, noticed kids were fascinated by hacking but didn't understand online safety.

2021

First Prototype

We created our first interactive lessons about password safety and online privacy. Kids loved the pixel-themed design and game-like approach!

2022

Growing Community

RoboPig expanded to 100+ schools and launched our first coding courses. We reached our first 1,000 young learners!

2023

Platform Launch

We launched the full RoboPig platform with three complete courses and interactive challenges. Our community grew to 5,000+ kids worldwide!

2024

The Future

We're developing new courses in AI safety, game development, and advanced cybersecurity. Our goal: reach 100,000 kids by 2025!
